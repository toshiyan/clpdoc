apstool – angular power spectrum calculation¶

Multipole binning¶

mytestw.qtt(lmax, rL, fC, Tlm1, Tlm2, nside=0)¶

Test

e.g., glm, clm = curvedsky.rec_lens.qtt(lmax,rL,fC,Tlm1,Tlm2[,nside])

Args:
  • lmax (int) : maximum multipole
  • rL (int) : minimum rL[0] and maximum multipoles rL[1] of CMB, with bounds (2)
  • fC (double): temperature angular power spectrum, with bounds (rL[1])
  • Tlm1 (dcmplx): 1st temperature alm, with bounds (rL[1]+1,rL[1]+1)
  • Tlm2 (dcmplx): 2nd temperature alm, with bounds (rL[1]+1,rL[1]+1)
Args (optional):
  • nside (int) : Nside for the convolution calculation, default to nside=0
Returns:
  • glm (dcmplx) : CMB lensing potential, with bounds (rL[1]+1,rL[1]+1)
  • clm (dcmplx) : curl mode (pseudo lensing potential), with bounds (rL[1]+1,rL[1]+1)
